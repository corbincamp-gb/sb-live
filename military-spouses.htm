Attention Prospective SkillBridge Industry Partners!

DOD is implementing important updates to the DOD SkillBridge partner application process to better manage and support our partnerships. On 31 July 2024 the Department ended rolling enrollment for new Industry Partner applications.

Effective 1 October 2024 DOD SkillBridge will transition to structured application enrollment periods.

  • Fall Enrollment Period: 1 October – 1 December
  • Spring Enrollment Period: 1 February – 1 April

    • DOD SkillBridge Authorized Organizations MOUs are automatically extended through 30 June 2025; MOU renewals will be processed prior to the 30 June 2025 blanket expiration date.

    Military Spouses

    Overview

    DOD SkillBridge partners may also offer job training, employment skills training, apprenticeship, and internship opportunities to military spouses.

    Many DOD SkillBridge partners recognize the value of training and hiring a military spouse. DOD policy dictates that DOD SkillBridge opportunities are for eligible transitioning military service members within 180-days of their separation from active duty, but many of the DOD SkillBridge authorized partners may also offer pathways to training and employment for veterans, National Guard and Reserve members, and military spouses.

    Military spouses are encouraged to explore the SkillBridge locations page and search for the keyword "spouse" to view partners that have indicated a desire to assist military spouses. Military spouse participation is not supported financially by DOD, but organizations may offer unique programs to military spouses.

    Military spouses interested in growing their educational and employment opportunities should explore the DOD's Spouse Education and Career Opportunities (SECO). SECO offers free career coaching services to military spouses in:

    • Career Exploration
    • Education, Training and Licensing
    • Employment Readiness
    • Career Connections

    SECO also offers coaching packages and financial scholarships to eligible spouses pursuing education to gain employment in a career field. Find out more by visiting myseco.militaryonesource.mil/portal or contacting the SECO Spouse Career Center by calling Military OneSource at 800-342-9647.
